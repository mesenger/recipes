Ingredients:
- 1 spoonful of Miso of any kind
- 2 carrots
- 1 onion
- 1 half of Hokaido pumpkin
- 1 litr of water
- some turmeric


Directions:
1. Peel and cut the vegetables
2. Boil the water
3. Put vegetables and turmeric into water and let it cook for 10 minutes
4. In the meantime take some boiling water, put it into small bowl and mix with miso.
5. After 10 minutes of boiling lower the flame and put miso into the pot.
6. let it simmer for 1 minute without cover and then turn of the flame.
7. let it finish on its own for about 10 minutes of more.
