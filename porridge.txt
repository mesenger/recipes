Ingredients:
- 1 cup of Rice Flakes
- some dried coconout
- some Goji berries
- little bit of various seeds
- half a litre of Rice milk
- some turmeric


Directions:
1. Put everything into pan.
2. Pour in the water.
3. Let it stand for some time.
4. Put the fire on, and cook it for 15-30 minutes, stirring occasionally and adding salt and cinammon.
